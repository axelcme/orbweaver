How we work

Build soil, not dependencies

Healthy soil grows healthy plants. We focus on building living soil that gets better each year, without fertilizers.

Plant for the long term

We choose plants that want to grow here, from seeds that were born here. They'll need less water and care once established.

Welcome wildlife

A balanced garden has predators for every pest. We design habitats that attract and shelter beneficial insects, reptiles, and birds.

Capture rain where it falls

Using simple earthworks and smart plantings that slow and sink water, we reduce or eliminate irrigation needs and prevent erosion.

Quiet work
